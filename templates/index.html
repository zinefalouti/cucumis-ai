ABOUT

Cucumis Melo uses “Computer Vision” to scan images and diagnose plants to check for diseases. This AI model was trained on 230 scenarios covering fungal, viral, and bacterial diseases, where 20% were sliced for evaluation and 80% for training and were annotated manually. This model not only classifies but also checks the details within each frame/image you scan.
